INDO NARA METAL W.L.L.

Your Reliable Metal Scrap Dealer for Enhanced Sustainability

Established in 2006, INDO NARA METAL W.L.L. is playing a pivotal role in the provision of high-quality metal scraps around the world. Based in Bahrain, we deal with all types of meta scraps, including ferrous and non-ferrous. Recognized as the largest, industrial-focused, high-volume metal scrap dealer, we supply a wide range of scrap materials such as Iron scrap HMS 1, HMS 1&2, Aluminum extrusions scrap, Aluminum scrap paint tabor scrap, Aluminum UBC scrap, Aluminum wire scrap, Aluminum tense scrap, Copper wire scrap, plastic regrind blue drum, plastic regrind IBC, used tyre TBR, 3 cut tyre scrap, OCC scrap, and more in bulk quantities. We also deals with Iron ore, Used Stainless steel pipes, Boxite, Cooking coal, Pig iron.


Looking for a ‘metal scrap yard near me?’ With Channel Partners and Yards in Bahrain, Europe, Saudi Arabia & USA, we implement environmentally friendly practices to segregate metal scraps while providing you with high-quality metal scrap and reducing waste. We also have a more stable and consistent supply of scrap materials, which makes us a reliable source of scrap metal for industrial and commercial operations. As one of the best scrap dealers, we have loading ports in the Middle East, Africa, Europe and USA which enhances our ability to reach diverse markets across the globe.

About Company

With Channel Partners and Yards in Bahrain, Europe, Saudi Arabia & USA, we have the infrastructure to handle large-scale operations. Our services include auction teams, collection services, and the ability to undertake demolition projects. We also offer convenient shipping terms, with Cost, Insurance, and Freight (CIF) as our preferred option. When it comes to payment, we accept a Telegraphic Transfer (TT) and a Letter of Credit (LC) at sight (irrevocable) to ensure secure transactions. If you are interested in supplying/purchasing all types of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap, plastic regrind, tire scrap, or paper scrap, please provide us with detailed specifications, origin, competitive price, loading port, certificates, photos, and videos. If you are a buyer, share your scrap requirements and destination port. We value long-term business partnerships and look forward to establishing a reliable and mutually beneficial relationship with you.

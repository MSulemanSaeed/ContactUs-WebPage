About us

Your Reliable Metal Scrap Dealer for Enhanced Sustainability

Established in 2006, INDO NARA METAL W.L.L. is playing a pivotal role in the provision of high-quality metal scraps around the world. Based in Bahrain, we deal with all types of meta scraps, including ferrous and non-ferrous. Recognized as the largest, high-volume metal scrap dealer, we supply a wide range of scrap materials such as Iron scrap HMS 1, HMS 1&2, Aluminum extrusions scrap, Aluminum scrap paint tabor scrap, Aluminum UBC scrap, Aluminum wire scrap, Aluminum tense scrap, Copper wire scrap, plastic regrind blue drum, plastic regrind IBC, used tyre TBR, 3 cut tyre scrap, OCC scrap, and more in bulk quantities. We also deals with Iron ore, Used Stainless steel pipes, Boxite, Cooking coal, Pig iron.

Looking for a ‘metal scrap yard near me?’ With Channel Partners and Yards in Bahrain, Europe, Saudi Arabia & USA, we implement environmentally friendly practices to segregate metal scraps with high-quality metal scrap and reducing waste. We also have a more stable and consistent supply of scrap materials, which makes us a reliable source of scrap metal for industrial and commercial operations. As one of the best scrap dealers, we have loading ports in the Middle East, Africa, Europe and USA.
